INITIATIVES TAKEN BY SARC TEAM

ASMP

Alumni Student Mentorship Program is, as the name suggests, a mentorship program wherein alumni are the mentors to current students. It provides the alumnus with a platform to interact with the students on a one to one basis and stay connected with the Institute...

Year Book

SARC initiated the institute yearbook for the final year students so that they can have their cherished memories at one place and reminisce even years after they graduate from the institute, and relive those moments in the words and pictures of their friends.

EVENTS OF SARC TEAM

ALUMNIATION

Alumination is the flagship event of SARC organized annually. The main motive is to bring alumni and students together for a span of 2 days and have an exchange of ideas, experiences and learning.

Read More

REUNIONS

SARC helps the IIT Bombay Alumni Association in the yearly reunions of various batches held in the month of December. A lot of alumni come back to the Institute to relive a few moments of friendship.

Read More

SARCASM

So you think you know IIT Bombay in and out? SARCASM provides you the chance to prove your mettle there in an online cryptic quiz with questions exclusively about IIT Bombay. The winners get exciting prizes! The dates of this years SARCASM would be out soon.

CORE TALKS

Core Talks is a series of talks held in order to help students get insight into the core world of their departments. It provides them with the opportunity of getting the first-hand review from the experts, our very own alumni who have established themselves in these fields so that they can make better career choices
